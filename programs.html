ESL Programs in Massachusetts

International Institute of New England

  • Offers free or low-cost ESL (ESOL) classes for immigrants and refugees
  • Focuses on workplace English skills and job readiness
  • Helps students gain independence and integrate into society
  • Provides additional support like job training and career services
  • Serves communities across Massachusetts

iine.org
English At Large

  • Provides free one-on-one ESL tutoring with trained volunteers
  • Focuses on personalized learning based on student needs
  • Helps immigrants build confidence in speaking and understanding English
  • Offers small group conversation classes
  • Emphasizes community integration and relationships

englishatlarge.org
Boston Public Library

  • Offers free ESL classes for adults
  • Provides conversation groups to practice speaking
  • Includes literacy programs for beginners
  • Accessible to anyone in the community
  • Helps learners improve everyday communication skills

bpl.org
UMass Boston

  • Offers structured ESL programs and intensive English courses
  • Prepares students for college-level education
  • Provides workplace communication training
  • Supports both international students and local residents
  • Includes academic and professional development opportunities
umb.edu
Boston Community Education Center

  • Provides free ESOL classes to underserved populations
  • Reports waitlists of up to 2+ years for some programs
  • Focuses on helping students find jobs and continue education
  • Offers beginner to advanced level classes
  • Emphasizes real-life English skills
esolcenterboston.org
Massachusetts Department of Education

  • Supports Adult Basic Education (ABE) and ESL programs statewide
  • Provides funding for ESL programs in Massachusetts
  • Reports that demand for ESL services is high
  • Works to improve access for underserved communities
  • Promotes workforce development through language education
