About

This website was created as part of the Lincoln-Sudbury Global Scholars Program Capstone Project. My topic focuses on ESL (English as a Second Language) opportunities for underprivileged communities in Massachusetts, exploring how language barriers impact access to education, jobs, and everyday life.

Through independent research, I looked at local ESL programs, the challenges many immigrants and low-income individuals face, and possible solutions to improve access. While this project is centered on Massachusetts, it reflects a broader global issue of language access and inequality, highlighting the importance of education in helping people build more stable and successful lives.
